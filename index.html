Takamasa Ishihara, better known as Miyavi, is a Japanese singer, songwriter, record producer, and actor known for his finger-slapping guitar playing style.

 

He has been involved since 1999, first as a guitarist for the now-disbanded visual kei rock band Dué le quartz and then as a solo artist since 2002. He became a part of the rock supergroup S.K.I.N. in 2007 and formed his own company, J-glam, in 2009. He has toured the world many times, with more than 250 shows in 30 countries as of 2015.
